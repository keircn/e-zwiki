E-Z.host is a powerful image and file hosting service that allows you to upload, share, and customize how your files are displayed across platforms like Discord.

## Getting Started

Configure your hosting settings at [e-z.gg/dash](https://e-z.gg/dash) and start uploading with our extensive customization options.

## Key Features

- **Custom Embeds** - Design how your uploads appear on Discord with custom titles, descriptions, and colors
- **Multiple Domains** - Choose from a variety of domains or use your own custom domain
- **Flexible URLs** - Customize filenames, paths, and subdomains
- **Cross-Platform Tools** - Native upload tools for every major platform
- **Discord Integration** - Custom webhooks for upload notifications
- **CDN Distribution** - All uploads are distributed via Cloudflare CDN
