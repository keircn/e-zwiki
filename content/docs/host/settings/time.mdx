---
title: Time Settings
description: Configure date and time format preferences for your uploads
---

Time Settings let you customize how dates and times are displayed in your upload embeds and timestamps.

## Country

Select your country from the dropdown. This helps determine the default date format and timezone options available to you.

## Timezone

Choose your timezone to ensure timestamps on your uploads are displayed in your local time.

## Date Format

Choose how dates are displayed:

| Format         | Example    |
| -------------- | ---------- |
| **MM/DD/YYYY** | 12/23/2025 |
| **DD/MM/YYYY** | 23/12/2025 |

## Time Format

Choose how times are displayed:

| Format            | Example |
| ----------------- | ------- |
| **24 Hour Clock** | 14:30   |
| **12 Hour Clock** | 2:30 PM |

## Saving Changes

Click **Save Changes** to apply your time settings configuration.
