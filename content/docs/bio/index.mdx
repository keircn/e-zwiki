## Key Features

- **Customizable URLs** - Choose your perfect bio URL (premium users get unicode support)
- **Rich Media Support** - Upload backgrounds, avatars, banners, and audio
- **Social Integration** - Connect Discord, social media, and custom links
- **Advanced Animations** - Tilt effects, cursor trails, and animated titles
- **Layout Flexibility** - Drag-and-drop component ordering
- **Real-time Discord Presence** - Show your live Discord status
- **Media Embeds** - Integrate external media content
- **Much more** - What are you waiting for
